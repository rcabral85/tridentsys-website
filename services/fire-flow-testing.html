NFPA 291 Fire Flow Testing

Professional on‑site testing with calibrated equipment. Deliverables include a PDF compliance report, water supply curve, and GIS exports (GeoJSON/KML).

Scope of Work

  • Site safety and OTM Book 7 traffic control (as required)
  • Static, residual, and pitot pressure measurements
  • Orifice coefficient application (C) and outlet size (d) validation
  • Available flow at 20 psi (Q20) and hydrant classification per NFPA 291
  • GPS and photos for documentation

Deliverables

  • PDF report with water supply curve and test details
  • CSV data export for records
  • GIS deliverables: GeoJSON/KML for Esri/QGIS
  • Optional: Summary memo for council/insurance

Turnaround

  • Standard: 2–3 business days after site work
  • Rush: 24 hours (subject to availability)
  • Multi‑site projects scoped with timeline
