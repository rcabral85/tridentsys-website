# Trident Systems Vision

## North Star

**Transform water utility operations through integrated asset management, GIS intelligence, field operations, regulatory compliance, and AI-driven analytics.**

## Mission Statement

Eliminate manual processes, reduce operational costs, and improve public safety by providing water utilities with a comprehensive platform that integrates seamlessly into their existing workflows.

## Strategic Pillars

### 1. **Asset Management Excellence**
- Complete lifecycle management for hydrants, pipelines, valves, and infrastructure
- Condition-based maintenance and replacement planning
- Real-time asset performance monitoring and analytics

### 2. **GIS Intelligence**
- Precise mapping and geospatial data analytics
- Integration with municipal GIS systems (Esri, QGIS)
- Spatial optimization for operations and planning

### 3. **Field Operations**
- Mobile-first field data collection with offline capabilities
- Real-time synchronization between field and office
- Streamlined workflows for inspections, testing, and maintenance

### 4. **Regulatory Compliance**
- Automated compliance tracking for NFPA 291, O. Reg 169/03
- Audit-ready documentation and reporting
- Proactive deadline management and notifications

### 5. **Predictive Analytics & AI**
- Machine learning for asset failure prediction
- Optimal maintenance scheduling and resource allocation
- Investment planning and budget optimization
