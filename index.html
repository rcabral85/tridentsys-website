Our NFPA 291 & OTM Book 7 Compliant Services

We specialize in Fire Flow Testing following NFPA 291 and implement OTM Book 7 compliant traffic control for municipal and private hydrant systems. Our comprehensive testing ensures your water distribution system meets critical fire protection requirements and that public safety is maintained during testing operations.

Traffic Control & Safety (OTM Book 7)

  • Site-specific temporary traffic control layouts based on roadway type and posted speed
  • Use of approved signage, cones/drums, tapers and buffer space per OTM Book 7 tables
  • Competent worker setup/removal procedures and flagging as required
  • Coordination with road authorities for permits and lane impacts
